Chinook?

What is Chinook?
Chinook is a tool used to tell the community about time spent volunteering.
Who made Chinook?
A really awesome person named Jacob Guenther made Chinook.
Who uses Chinook?
You use it! Chinook is a tool made for the public to use.
What does Chinook do?
You can think of Chinook as a blog, made by the community. You fill out and add forms about your time volunteering.
I volunteered somewhere!
Cool! Tell the community about it by pressing "Add" in the menu bar!
What happens if I add an entry?
Your entry gets sent to the Chinook email. From the data sent, I check the form for any profanity or far-fetched claims.
What happens next?
If your form is assessed positively, then it will be posted on the entries section of Chinook!
I have a question!
Great! Send all of your inquiries to chinook.contact@gmail.com. I will try to answer as soon as possible!
